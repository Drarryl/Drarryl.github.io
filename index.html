Regret

Date: 09-12-2020

A wise man once said... "Only the heart knows how to find what is precious". But how far can we listen to our heart, before we start to spiral down a rabbit hole deeper than we could even imagine, deeper than that of a black hole. Now, that right there is quite the concept, something that is so famously known for being a mysterious entity, yet we haven't truly discovered what makes it so great. What makes it something so beautiful, yet so confusing. What built it up from nothing, and made it something. That, is something that should never be taken for granted; the unimaginable journey of creating something, out of nothing. And now I leave you with this, Is happiness something that you can see? Or, is it something made up for you to perceive the world in some different type of way?
